Hotel Albatros (Seattle, WA)

Nachos are great, but Tatchos are a step above in terms of greatness. They are that same wonderful combination of carbs with cheese, sour cream and veggies but the nachos chips are replaced by a better carb.

What Hotel Albatross has to offer for totchos is legit. Price was on the high side, but I think that everything is too expensive at all time so perhaps you should disregard my opinion on this. The combination of ingredients was good, the texture of the tots (and the way they were fried) was as good as it gets. It came with a good combination of stuff and they had a legit happy hour that included good discounts on shitty bear!

  • Setting: 3/5
  • Tot quality: 5/5
  • Price: $$$

Washington State Ferry

Ferries are some of the greatest things on Earth (in my opinion) and Washington State has a good network of kick-ass ferries. With kick-ass ferries comes kick-ass cafeterias. In the WSF you can find all sort of pretty good enough food but my go to on just about every single WSF ride, are the T-O-T-S. To be fair, they are not that great. I did not grow up with tot but I heard tales from lucky kids that had those in public schools. WSF's tots are probably exactly what I imagine public school tots to be like. Not great... BUT, the scenery and the rocky boat makes up for it all!

Tots are good-enough, the crispiness score would be low, the flavor score would also be low, but they are cheap and you can be sitting on your butt eating tots while being driven around the beautiful San Juan islands. Not too bad.

  • Setting: 5/5
  • Tot quality: 2/5
  • Price: $
